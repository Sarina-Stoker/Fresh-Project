Line Dancing Club at IIT

One of my favorite hobbies is line dancing. When I say that, most people think of something close to square dancing, but it is not that at all! My culture has urbanized the term and it is now much closer to actual choreography using current pop, r&b and hip-hop music. I started when I was 15 years old and 5 years later I am still going strong. The only problem is, I dont have a permanent group to enjoy it with. I say "permanent" because I have gotten some friends together a few times to teach them a line dance for fun, but I don't have a committed group. I think it is really fun and I have faith that many others will learn to love it too. My goal is to find others that are interested so that we can start a club!
